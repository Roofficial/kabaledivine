Admission Process

01

Inquiry & School Visit

Contact us to schedule a personal tour and experience our faith-filled learning environment.

02

Submit Application

Complete the application form and submit required documents (birth certificate, previous reports if applicable).

03

Assessment & Interview

Age-appropriate assessment and parent/guardian interview to ensure the right fit.

04

Enrollment Confirmation

Pay registration fees and secure your child’s place once accepted.

Required Documents

  • Copy of child's Birth Certificate
  • 4 recent passport-size photographs of the child
  • Latest report card or transfer certificate (if transferring)
  • Immunization / vaccination records
