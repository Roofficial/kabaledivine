Students Engaged in Learning

Our Balanced Curriculum

We follow the Uganda National Examinations Board (UNEB) syllabus while enriching it with life skills, creative arts, ICT, and character education — ensuring every child grows academically, spiritually, and socially.

Nursery Level (Baby – Top Class)

Focus on foundational literacy, numeracy, play-based learning, life skills, creative arts, and Christian religious education.

Lower Primary (P.1 – P.3)

Core subjects: Literacy I & II, Numeracy, English, Mathematics, Religious Education, Local Language (Runyankore/Rukiga), and creative activities.

Upper Primary (P.4 – P.7)

Comprehensive subjects: English, Mathematics, Integrated Science, Social Studies & Religious Education, plus Art & Crafts, Physical Education, and preparation for PLE.
